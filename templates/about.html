About BusRush — APSRTC

🎯 Problem

APSRTC commuters in Guntur and Tenali districts have no way to know how crowded a bus will be before leaving home. This causes missed buses, uncomfortable journeys, and poor planning.

💡 Solution

BusRush uses Machine Learning to predict rush levels and shows exactly how many passengers travel between every stop — so commuters can choose the least crowded boarding point.

👥 Passenger Count Feature

Our unique feature shows an Origin-Destination matrix — passenger counts between every pair of stops on your route. Board at the least crowded stop for a comfortable journey.
